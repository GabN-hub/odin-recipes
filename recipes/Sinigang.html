Sinigang

Description

Sinigang is a Filipino soup or stew characterized by its sour and savory taste. It is most often associated with tamarind, although it can use other sour fruits and leaves as the souring agent. It is one of the more popular dishes in Filipino cuisine. The soup is usually accompanied by rice.

Ingredients

  • Meat of choice
  • Tamarind
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Vegetables of choice

Steps

  1. Put water and the pork in a cooking pot and boil
  2. Put the tomatoes, red onion, and fih sauce then cover and boil until pork is tender
  3. To the mixture, put the taro and cook for 5 minutes
  4. Add other hard vegetables
  5. Add the tamarind and season with salt
  6. Put the kangkong leaves then turn off the heat immediately.
